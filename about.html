About Our System

In a world where digital identities are constantly at risk, verifying whether a face is real or spoofed has become essential. Our integrated Anti-Spoofing System ensures the authenticity of every facial interaction by detecting fake, replayed, or deepfake inputs with precision.

We combine deep learning models powered by MobileNetV3, ResNet50, and custom CNN architectures trained to differentiate between real and spoofed faces across images, videos, and live webcam feeds. This multi-model integration allows real-time verification, even under challenging conditions.Our mission is to build a secure and reliable face verification system that stops fake or replayed identities in real time.
